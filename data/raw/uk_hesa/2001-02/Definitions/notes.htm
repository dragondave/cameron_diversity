4. From August 2001 Homerton College return staff data and finance data from the School of Health Studies only. All other staff and finance are now included in the figures for The University of Cambridge.
5. There was no staff data submitted for Northern College of Education in the 2001/02 staff return
6. There was no staff data submitted for UHI Millennium Institute in the 2001/02 staff return.
7. The (Deficit)/Surplus is on continuing operations after depreciation of fixed assets at valuation and before exceptional items, tax and minority interest.
8. Previous year's figures used for comparison are as reported to HESA in 2000/01 rather than as contained in re-stated financial statements.
9. The funding council grants for HE provision include SHEFC grants for all provision.
10. The funding council grants for FE provision are not applicable to SHEFC.
11. Staff whose ethnicity is not known includes those who have been coded 'information refused' and 'information not yet sought'.
12. As a consequence of a review of cost centres by funding councils, additional cost centres were introduced in 1997/98. Use of the new cost centres was compulsory for institutions in England and Northern Ireland in 1998/99. In addition, cost centre 15 (General sciences) and cost centre 22 (Other technologies) were not used by institutions in England and Northern Ireland from 1998/99.
13. For the 1999/2000 year HESA issued extra guidance to institutions in relation to the grade field, which resulted in some institutions changing their reporting practice. This guidance was also issued for 2000/01 and 2001/02, and although it appears that there has been no significant effect on reporting practices in these years, care should be taken when undertaking time series analysis by grade.
14. Average age was calculated on actual age (as opposed to age groups) for staff with known age.
15. Finance data in 2001/02 for The Northern College of Education is for 4 months up to 30 November 2001.
16. Where comparisons are made between the latest financial year 2001/02 and the previous year 2000/01, the previous year's restated figures do not include the Conservatoire of Dance and Drama.
17. Where comparisons are made between the latest financial year 2001/02 and the previous year 2000/01, the previous year's restated figures do include UHI Millennium Institute, however it should be noted that these figures cover the financial year 1 April 2000 to 31 March 2001 rather than the period 1 August 2001 to 31 July 2002 as followed by the other institutions.
18. Finance data in 2001/02 for UHI Millennium Institute is for 16 months, from 1 April 2001 to 31 July 2002.

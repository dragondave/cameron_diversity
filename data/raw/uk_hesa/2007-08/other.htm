Other publishers

Other statistical publishers include:

The Stationery Office publishes national statistics on behalf of National Statistics, including a volume of Education Statistics for the United Kingdom.

The Department for Innovation, Universities and Skills (DIUS) publishes a series of statistical bulletins concerning education.

Similarly the Higher Education Funding Council for England (HEFCE), the Scottish Executive (Scottish Government), the Scottish Further and Higher Education Funding Council (SFC), the Welsh Assembly Government (WAG), the Higher Education Funding Council for Wales (HEFCW) and the Department for Employment and Learning, Northern Ireland (DEL(NI)) publish statistical bulletins some of which are concerned with higher education.

UCAS publishes statistics concerning the application and admission process.

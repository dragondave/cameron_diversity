Changes from 2008/09 to 2009/10

As part of our continued commitment to enhance the data that we supply, we have revised the Resources publication content into separate Staff and Finance publications.

To assist you locating staff data in the new table structure, we have detailed below the table listing from 2008/09 and where the comparable data can be found this year.

Should you have any queries please do not hesitate to contact information.provision@hesa.ac.uk.
